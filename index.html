The host

The story of Count Dracula is a story of self-improvement. A man who overcame a terrible condition that still privates him from digesting regular food and can only survive thanks to the generosity of blood donors. He left behind those days of youth doing businesses with the Devil and since then he has been seeking redemption helping others.

His companies provide employment to thousands of people around the world and he regularly cooperates with local nonprofit organisations. The wellbeing of the local communities in Transylvania is his number one priority and this hotel gives testimony of it.
